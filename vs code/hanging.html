hanger planter.

This elegant hanging plant pot is a perfect addition to any indoor or outdoor space. Made from high-quality ceramic, the pot features a sleek, minimalist design with a glossy finish that complements a variety of decor styles. The pot measures 8 inches in diameter and 5 inches in height, providing ample space for small to medium-sized plants. It comes with a sturdy macrame rope hanger, which is adjustable to fit different ceiling heights. The pot also includes a drainage hole at the bottom, ensuring proper water flow and preventing root rot. Ideal for displaying succulents, trailing vines, or small flowers, this hanging plant pot adds a touch of greenery and style to your home or garden.
